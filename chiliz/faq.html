What are the delivery times?

Depending on your location, the time for delivery takes 2 to 10 working days.

Where is the clothing produced?

You can download the CSR report of our clothing partner here.

To which countries do you ship?

Worldwide with the exception of Ukraine, Russia and Belarus.

How can I know the right size?

Available sizes are included in the product description for each item. There is also a size guide on product level and a video available how to measure your size.

What if my measurements are in between sizes?

If you want a loose fit, choose the size higher. If you want a tight fit, choose the size lower.

What can I do if an item/size is out of stock?

We’re aiming to ensure there’s enough merch available for everyone to get what they want, but, if an item isn’t available, please pop an email to danika@chiliz.com and we’ll do our best to get it for you.
