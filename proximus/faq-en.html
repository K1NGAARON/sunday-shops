How do I know the right size?

At product level, there is a size chart and also a video available on how to measure your size. All products are unisex and fit true to size (size S - XXL).

What should I do if I am between sizes?

If you are between sizes, you can choose the larger size if you want a looser fit, if you want a tighter fit, you can choose the smaller size.

What to do with returns?

Contact shop.support@wardrobe.teamsunday.com where someone will help you further with your return.

Where is the clothing produced?

All clothing is produced in Łódź in Poland.

What about VAT?

All prices on the webshop include VAT.

Questions about data protection?

All data remains confidential and complies with GDPR legislation.

Which countries do you deliver to?

We deliver only in Belgium.

How long will it take to receive my item?

You should receive everything within a week. As soon as the products are shipped, you will receive a track & trace on the e-mail address mentioned in your order.

What can I do if an item/size is sold out?

Please mention this in your e-mail: shop-support@wardrobe.teamsunday.com. We will collect the data and take it into account in case of restocking.

What payment options are available?

You can pay via bancontact & Visa/Mastercard.

Who can I contact in case of problems?

Please email: shop-support@wardrobe.teamsunday.com. We will reply soon.
