Who is Sunday?

This is our partner. It’s a Belgian company from Roeselare and a big player on the market of qualitative branded clothing. They adapted our designs and produced the clothing. Sunday is known for qualitative products and has a great webshop platform that also handles the payments and distribution of the clothes for us.

Is this sustainable?

Yes. All the clothes are made in Europe and sustainability is a high priority for our supplier. You can read their sustainability report if you want more information.

Where is the clothing produced?

All clothing is produced in Łódź in Poland.

Questions about data protection?

All data remains confidential and complies with GDPR legislation.
