How do I return my package?

The items provided are offered for free. Therefore we don't offer a retour service.

What if your parcel gets lost?

We are dependent on courier services for the delivery of packages. You will receive updates from the courier service regarding your package via mail. If it gets lost or it can't be delivered, normally it gets sent back to the supplier that will send it to the office.In exceptional cases, it can occur that the package is untraceable and can't be retrieved.

When will the packages be delivered?

We are aiming to have everything delivered during the 2nd half of December and in the month of January.

What happens if you don't collect your parcel from the courier or parcel shop?

The package gets sent back to the supplier of the items which is located Poland. They will send it to the office in January.

How can I know the right size?

The provided products are one size fits all.
