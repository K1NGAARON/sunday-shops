Who is Sunday?

This is our partner. It’s a Belgian company from Roeselare and a big player on the market of qualitative branded clothing. They adapted our designs and produced the clothing. Sunday is known for qualitative products and has a great webshop platform that also handles the payments and distribution of the clothes for us.

Where do the products come from, and are they sustainable?

All products are produced in Europe, using only sustainable and/or recycled materials.

What’s the benefit of picking up my gift at the office?

By picking up your gift at the office instead of having it delivered at your door, we are able to cut the carbon emissions resulting from the required transport by 30 %.

What about the packaging?

By encouraging all people of delaware to pick up their gifts in one of the delaware offices, we can limit the required packaging to a bare minimum: only the outer boxes instead of individual packages. Even so all packaging, be it delivered to the office or to your door, will be made from recycled materials.

By when should I place my order?

Place your order between October 1st and October 10th. If you are unable to place your order during this time, your personal budget will be donated to the Food Banks so you will still be contributing to a good cause!

When can I pick up my gift?

If you (or a friend) are picking up your gift in one of the delaware offices, this can be done anytime between Monday December 16th and Friday December 20th. Details as to where exactly you can pick up the gifts will be provided in the coming weeks.
If picking up your gift at one of the offices (yourself or via a friend) is not possible and you’ve opted for a direct delivery, you will receive a separate email with shipment details including the expected delivery date.

What about GDPR?

All personal details will be removed following the delivery of the products in the second half of December.
