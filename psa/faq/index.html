To which countries do you deliver?

We ship worldwide with the exception of Russia, Belarus & Ukraine for obvious reasons.

How long does it take to receive my item?

Generally this takes 2 to 10 working days, depending on the destination.

What can I do if an item / size is sold out?

Please mention this to psashop@gmail.com. We gather the data and try to restock as soon as possible.

What if my measurements are in between sizes?

If your measurement is between sizes, you can choose to go for the bigger size if you prefer a looser fit, if you prefer a tighter fit, you can opt to go for the smaller size.

How can I know the right size?

At product level, there is a size guide and also a video available how you can measure your size.

What if my package gets delivered damaged?

Please contact psashop@gmail.com. Then we can look into the matter and get back to you asap with more information.

What if I want to retour my package?

Please send it to 26 Southgate Apartments DroghedaMeath (IE)A92 YF84 Ireland. There is a 14-day window to return packages, counting from the day that it has been delivered according to the track & trace code. Once we have received the items in an undamaged state, we will refund the money asap. PSA is not responsible for retour costs.

What if my package gets lost?

Please contact psashop@gmail.com. Then we can look into the matter and get back to you asap with more information.

Do I have to pay custom duties or extra taxes?

PSA takes care of this cost. PSA holds the right to change this at any time.
