What products are there available?

The X1F Fashion Line currently includes hoodies, sweaters, polos and t-shirts in various colors. We are working on offering you more pieces or a larger variety of colors in the future. Since we have to have the clothes pre-produced, we will start with this selection and expand the collection bit by bit.
If you have concrete wishes for more colors, please leave them here on the X1F SharePoint.

How to request your FREE item?

If you haven't ordered a free piece yet, you can do so at any time via the webshop. Just contact us by email and we will send you a voucher with which you can order a free piece from the X1F Fashion collection.
When purchasing additional items , you can pay by credit card, PayPal, Klarna or SEPA bank transfer. The prices of all clothes correspond to the purchase prices including VAT, we pass the prices on to you of course 1:1. Shipping to the offices or private addresses will be paid by X1F.

How can I know the right size

At product level, there is a size guide and also a video available how you can measure your size. All products are unisex and fit true to size (size S - XXXL)

What if my measurements are in between sizes

If your measurement is between sizes, you can choose to go for the bigger size if you prefer a looser fit, if you prefer a tighter fit, you can opt to go for the smaller size.

What to do with returns?

If the item you purchased really doesn't fit, you can pass it on to customers, family members or friends and request a voucher via email to order the same item of clothing in a different size.

Please note that returns are very expensive and in the interest of sustainability and in view of the costs involved, we should all try to avoid them.

We hope you understand that we can not offer a return for the free pieces, because we, unlike large webshops, have no capacity for logistics, etc. If your free part really does not fit, please try to exchange it with a colleague in the interest of the group.
