To which countries do you deliver?

We currently ship within the EU, the US and Canada as part of a test for the Asphalt brand. After we review the results and in celebration of Asphalt's 20th anniversary, we’ll do our best to expand shipping worldwide.

What is a pre-registration?

As mentioned on the product pages, we’ll be doing a pre-registration for this EU drop. The website will open in early October and remain available for about a week. Once the pre-order period ends, we’ll begin production. After production is complete, your order will be shipped to you.

How long does it take to receive my item?

As mentioned above, we are using a pre-registration model. Once the limited-edition drop ends, we’ll produce the merch and ship it directly to your doorstep. You can expect a 1-2 month delivery timeframe after placing your order. Since this is a test to gauge player interest in Asphalt merch, we’ll aim to speed up delivery for the worldwide launch.

Why is shipping more expensive compared to large retailers?

As much as we’d love to offer fast and free shipping, we're a small-scale merch shop and can’t cover all the costs. To make up for this, we’ve set affordable prices, especially considering the top-tier quality of our products.

What is Asphalt brand?

Asphalt is a popular racing game franchise developed by Gameloft. Known for its high-quality gameplay, stunning graphics, and a wide selection of real-world cars, Asphalt offers players an immersive racing experience. Since its debut in 2004, the franchise has expanded across multiple platforms, including mobile devices, PCs, and consoles. With millions of players worldwide, Asphalt has become a leading name in racing games.

What if my measurements are in between sizes?

If your measurements fall between sizes, we recommend choosing the larger size for a looser fit, or the smaller size if you prefer a tighter fit. Our products generally fit true to size, except for items labeled ""oversized"" (as noted on the product page), which already have a loose fit—you can choose a smaller size for these.
