How does the Abbyy coin system work?
  1. You receive an invitation link to set up your personal account
  2. Within your account there will be 50 ABBYYCoins that you can spend
  3. You choose one of the three boxes and your preferred size
  4. You proceed to checkout and fill in your data
  5. Place the order and then sit back, relax, your package will arrive asap
  6. Wear your Abbyy swag with pride
Which products are available?

Within the ABBYY Fashion Line you can currently choose from a box with a hoodie, polo or t-shirt. With every box comes a tote bag, pen and notebook.

Which sizes are available?

All products are unisex and fit true to size. If you click on the products, you can see the available sizes.
In order to choose the right size, there is a video available how you can measure your size and a size guide down low. The clothes have a normal fit and are true to their size.

What if my measurements are in between sizes?

If your measurement is between sizes, you can choose to go for the bigger size if you prefer a looser fit, if you prefer a tighter fit, you can opt to go for the smaller size.

What to do with returns?

The items are being provided by Abbyy for free so a return service is not available.

What if the package gets lost?

Please send an email to XZY@ and we will look into it.

What about shipping costs & customs?

This is being taken care of by Abbyy to ensure that your packages gets delivered at your doorstep asap.
