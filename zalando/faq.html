Are these products on stock?

No, we make everything made to order.

What is the delivery date when we order?

This platform is made for requesting a quote and this is not an order yet. The average lead time is about 6 weeks, but this depends on the product, quantity and design. The Sunday project manager will inform you about the exact delivery date.

Am I placing an order on this platform?

No, we will send you an official quote for approval. Only after approving this quote, the order is confirmed.

Can I request a product that's not on the platform?

Certainly, using the contact page you can get in touch with us and we can help you design and create a totally new item.

How much is the shipping cost?

The final shipping cost will be calculated based on the # of boxes and location. This will be on the offer you receive.

Can I change the design of a product?

For the fixed collection, you can't change the design. All designs under the fixed collection tab have been carefully made while respecting the Zalando brand. These designs are also approved by the brand team and have been produced in the past.

For the customisable collection, you will be able to change certain elements of the design.

Can I order a smaller quantity?

For smaller quantities, please contact the project manager of Sunday under ‘contact. Sometimes this is possible.

How do I enter the sizes?

You can add the size breakdown during check out. There is a box available where you can add extra information.

This is not obligated and the size breakdown can also be discussed after the offer. This way you already know the total budget before putting in the effort of collecting all sizes. We can also assist with collecting the sizes (standard size breaks, size guides, …)

Is this sustainable?

Yes. All the clothes are made in Europe and sustainability is a high priority for our supplier. You can read their sustainability report here.

Who is “Sunday”?

This is our partner which we have teamed up with for several projects over the last year. They always provided an excellent service and they helped us with the designs, production of the clothing and are hosting this order platform.

Sunday is known for focussing on premium quality as they want that the Zalando items are actually worn/used.

You can have a look on their website here: teamsunday.com

I have a campaign in mind but I can't find the right item on the webshop?

Please contact the project manager of Sunday.
They are experts in providing guidance and bringing an idea to life.
