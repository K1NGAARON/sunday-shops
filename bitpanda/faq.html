Delivery

How does delivery work?

In order to ensure we are environmentally conscious, we deliver all packages to our key hubs for those who are located at or near a hub. If you are not based at a hub, please select the ‚ÄúOther location‚Äù option upon checkout and we will deliver to your home address.

Can I choose where my order is delivered?

Unfortunately not. There is a checking service which ensures packages are delivered to the location aligned with your Workday profile, therefore if you are assigned to our Vienna hub, your order will go to the office. Not to worry, you can swing by, pick it up and show off your new merch to other pandas!

Why am I being charged postage?

If you notice our Boutique is adding shipping costs on to your order, it means you have ordered too little. We want to ensure that shipping is not only cost efficient, but also ESG friendly. Therefore if all you want is a pair of socks, it might be worth waiting until the next launch, or adding something to your order.

What to do with returns?

As a general rule we do not provide a "returns" option. Please note that returns are very expensive and in the interest of sustainability and in view of the costs involved, we should all try to avoid them. Therefore we ask that you triple-check the sizing details and watch the sizing video before ordering.

We hope you understand that we can not offer a return for the free pieces, because we, unlike large webshops, have no capacity for logistics, etc. If your free part really does not fit, please try to exchange it with a colleague in #company-bitpanda-boutique.

General Questions

How often does the merchandise selection update?

We aim to keep things fresh with regular updates to our merchandise selection. Keep an eye on our Slack channel #company-bitpanda-boutique for new drop announcements!

How can I know the right size?

At product level, there is a size guide and also a video available which explains how you can measure your size. Some products are unisex and all fit true to EU size (size XS - XXL).

What if my measurements are in between sizes?

If your measurement is between sizes, you can choose to go for the bigger size if you prefer a looser fit, if you prefer a tighter fit, you can opt to go for the smaller size.

Why can't I add duplicates of the same item to my shopping basket?

As we have limited stock of these items, we think it is only fair that all Pandas have an opportunity to grab an item for themselves. Therefore, you are limited to only one item per product, i.e. one t-shirt, of a particular collection. However, if there is stock left during the next shopping window, and if you‚Äôre quick enough, you can grab another one!
